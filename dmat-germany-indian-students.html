dMAT — Germany's New Exam for Indian Master's Applicants

📅 30 June 2026 By Dr. Mushtaq Ali, KIT Engineering Commerce Business
← Back to all articles

Germany just changed the rules. On 29 June 2026, APS India officially announced the dMAT (Digital Master Test) — a new mandatory exam for selected Indian Master's applicants. The first exam date is 26 September 2026 and registration is already open. If you are planning to apply for Summer Semester 2027 or later, this is everything you need to know.

What Is the dMAT?

The dMAT (Digital Master Test) is a standardised academic aptitude test for Indian graduates applying to Master's programmes in Germany. It was developed by g.a.s.t. — the same organisation behind TestDaF and TestAS — in collaboration with German universities, and is jointly introduced with APS India.

It does not test memorised facts or formulas. Instead it measures:

  • Analytical thinking and reasoning ability
  • General cognitive skills (pattern recognition, logical thinking)
  • Application of subject knowledge — how you use what you know
🔑 Key Point

The dMAT is an additional requirement inside the APS process — it does not replace your APS certificate or TestAS. Both still apply where required.

Who Needs the dMAT?

You need the dMAT if all three of the following apply to you:

  • You are an Indian graduate (Bachelor's complete or nearly complete)
  • Your undergraduate degree is in Engineering (including Computer Science, IT), Commerce / Accounting / Finance / Economics, or Business / Management
  • You are applying for a German Master's programme for Summer Semester 2027 or later

Who Does NOT Need It

  • Bachelor's applicants
  • PhD applicants
  • Students in exchange, double-degree, or university partnership programmes
  • Degrees outside the three affected fields (pure science, arts, medicine, etc.)
  • Students applying for Winter Semester 2026/27 — first certificates only available October 2026

Interdisciplinary degrees? Fields like Engineering Management, Business Analytics, Industrial Engineering, Data Science, or FinTech may fall between categories. The final classification is based on the official APS India list. When in doubt, contact aps-india.de directly to confirm.

The Grandfather Rule — Are You Already Exempt?

If any of the following happened before 29 June 2026, you are exempt from dMAT for your current APS procedure:

  • You completed your APS online registration before 29 June 2026
  • You shipped your complete APS documents before 29 June 2026
  • You already received your APS certificate

This applies even if your APS verification is still ongoing. If a new APS application is submitted later, the rules at that time will apply.

Cost

The dMAT fee is €150, paid to g.a.s.t. during registration. This is on top of the regular APS fee. The good news: the certificate is valid indefinitely — you take the dMAT once and it never expires.

Test Structure — What You Actually Sit

The test takes approximately 3.5 hours including a break. It is conducted in English at approved test centres in India. All questions are single-choice format.

🧠
Core Module
General academic aptitude — no prior knowledge required. Tests how you think, not what you know.
  • Figure sequences & pattern recognition
  • Mathematical equations & quantitative reasoning
  • Latin squares & logical thinking
📚
Subject Module
Field-specific knowledge and application. For APS applicants in 2026: the General Academic Module.
  • Subject knowledge from your undergraduate field
  • Application of concepts to new problems
  • Analytical thinking within your discipline

How Scoring Works

Your dMAT certificate shows two scores:

Score TypeWhat It MeansRange
Percentile Rank% of test-takers who scored lower than you. Percentile 70 = you scored better than 70% of all participants.0–100
dMAT ScoreTotal correct answers converted to a standardised scale. Mean = 100. Reported separately for Core and Subject modules.0–200

There is no pass or fail. Universities decide individually how to use your dMAT results — some set minimum percentile cut-offs, others use it as one factor among several.

Updated APS Process — Where dMAT Fits

1
Take the dMAT
Register at d-mat.de → sit the exam (26 September 2026 first date) → receive certificate ~2 weeks later (from 12 October 2026)
2
Submit dMAT certificate + APS documents to APS India
APS India cannot complete the APS procedure until your dMAT certificate is included. All other APS documents must still be submitted as per the APS checklist.
3
APS India verifies your academic documents
Standard APS document verification process — transcripts, degree certificates, marksheets.
4
APS certificate issued
Your APS certificate will reference your dMAT result. Required before applying for a German student visa.
5
Apply to German universities → German student visa
University applications, admission decisions, blocked account, visa application — the standard process from here.

How to Prepare

Since dMAT is brand new, there are no official past papers yet. Based on the test structure described by g.a.s.t.:

Core Module

  • Practise figure sequence questions — IQ test-style abstract reasoning resources work well
  • Revise basic quantitative reasoning: arithmetic, algebra, equations
  • Practise Latin square puzzles — logic grid puzzles

Subject / General Academic Module

  • Revise core concepts from your undergraduate field
  • Focus on application of knowledge to new problems, not memorisation
  • All questions are in English — practise reading and solving in English

Practical Tips

  • Register immediately — seats at test centres are first-come, first-served
  • Take 26 September 2026 (the first date) to give maximum buffer before university deadlines
  • Budget 2–3 extra weeks in your overall timeline for the certificate to arrive
  • Check d-mat.de regularly for practice materials as they become available
